About Us

Welcome to Hanzade Restaurant

Nestled in the lively streets of Bradford, Hanzade is more than just a restaurant; it's a culinary destination — a place where food becomes legend.

We invite you to experience the rich flavors of Turkey and the Mediterranean, all prepared with the finest halal ingredients. From succulent steaks to traditional Turkish delicacies, all crafted with passion by our skilled chefs.

Step into our inviting ambiance and feel the warmth of Turkish hospitality. Whether you're craving the diverse flavors of Anatolian cuisine or the sizzle of a perfectly cooked steak, Hanzade Restaurant is here to exceed your expectations and create unforgettable dining memories.
